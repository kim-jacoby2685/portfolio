About Me

Hi, I'm Kim Jacoby, a UX designer and accessibility specialist with a passion for creating inclusive digital experiences.

I currently work as a Principal User Experience Designer and am pursuing a Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction at Iowa State University.

I have more than 15 years of experience creating inclusive digital experiences. My work focuses on accessibility, UX design, content strategy, and helping organizations build digital products that are usable by everyone.

My Background Includes

  • User experience design
  • Digital accessibility
  • Content strategy
  • Accessible document development
  • User research
  • Artificial intelligence in design
  • Front-end web development

I enjoy solving complex problems and creating experiences that are intuitive, useful, and accessible to everyone.
